UNILEVER adopts Bunker in 12 countries.

More than 3 years of trust and growing together.

We are currently celebrating the agreement with Unilever to use the Bunker platform in 12 LATAM countries. This consolidates the link between both companies that has been growing for the past 3 years.

This is why we are proud to tell you that one of the main advertisers in the world trusts Bunker to manage and control their digital ecosystem. The modules: Dashboard, Campaigns, Paid Media, Reporting, Consumers and Feelings make the Bunker platform a tool that simplifies and unifies information coming form social networks from each one of the 12 countries that conform the multinational in LATAM.

In each platform’s module, different parts of the organisation, responsible for different brands in different countries use it for their specific objectives and needs, but always using the same tool that covers almost 100% of the company’s digital ecosystem. This way, from the agency’s community managers that work their brands in each country, directors who buy media, brand managers to the high executives, utilise Bunker to create and monitor the digital plan and everything in one single place.
