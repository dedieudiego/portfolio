Process
  • User-Centered

    Healthcare should be focused first and foremost on people. That is why we parter with you to build solutions that put humans first and build digital products that improve their overall engagement and wellbeing.

  • Design Driven

    As a design agency at heart, we put special attention to the user experience, functionality flow and the visual design of your product. As Steve Jobs said, “The design is not just what it looks like and feels like. The design is how it works”.

  • Compliant & Connected

    We build secure digital healthcare products and pay special attention to HIPAA compliance. We also carefully analyze system integrations to make sure you product can securely connect with third party medical systems for an integrated workflow.

Our process
Strategy & validation

Through a discovery and validations process we find the best strategy for the product lifecycle

Design

At this stage we envision how the product user experience will be and how it will look like by testing it with real users.

Development

Once the development stage starts we will iterate with the design team to improve areas regarding the technology we will use.

Launch and test

We will set all the environment to launch the product and start testing right away to find unusual bugs.

Maintenance & Support

By collecting and analyzing feedback we will prepare the porduct to grow or improve areas to make it optimal.
