Xperiment

Collaborating with esteemed institutions like Berkeley, MIT, and Oxford, our push research project in AI endeavors to push the boundaries of knowledge and innovation in this critical field. Together, we aim to tackle pressing challenges and explore emerging frontiers.

GOALS

  • Catalyze the development of ethical frameworks to guide the responsible deployment of AI in society.
  • Pioneer breakthroughs in advanced machine learning algorithms, neural network architectures, and interdisciplinary applications of AI.
  • Foster collaborations among institutions to drive interdisciplinary research in AI.
  • Establish global leadership in AI research through collaborative efforts with Berkeley, MIT, and Oxford.

Xperiment will work with 3 leading institutions around the world

  • Explainable AI In Retail, Transport & Logistics
  • Deep Graph Networks in Travel, Transport & Logistics
  • Mutli-Agent Systems in Retail
Explainable AI In Retail, Transport & Logistics
Explainable AI (XAI) in retail and transport & logistics aims to create transparent decision-making systems.This transparency builds trust and enables human comprehension of AI-driven insights and recommendations, crucial for sectors where decision rationale is vital for operations and customer satisfaction.
Deep Graph Networks in Travel, Transport & Logistics
Utilizing advanced Deep GraphNetworks in travel, transport, and logistics domains entails leveraging intricate graph structures to enhance decision-making, optimize routes, and boost operational efficiency.
Mutli-Agent Systems in Retail
In retail, Multi-Agent Systems involve multiple intelligent agents collaborating to achieve specific objectives. These systems improve decision-making, inventory management, and customer interactions by leveraging collective intelligence within the retail environment.

Xplore

The undergraduate research program operates part-time during academic terms, seamlessly transitioning to full-time immersion during summers. Pre-doctoral programs aim to bolster research skills, increasing acceptance rates into advanced programs. Postdoctoral fellowships specifically target AI-focused research for KSA students.

GOALS

  • Promote experiential learning and foster interdisciplinary collaboration
  • Enhance research skills, critical thinking, and professional development.
  • Provide mentorship opportunities and contribute to ongoing research efforts.
